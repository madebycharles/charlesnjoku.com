Charles Njoku

I am what you call a 'Tech Swiss Army Knife' or 'Generalist'. I am constanly at the nexus of using my varied skill set to solve problems. My experience range from designing brands and marketing campaigns to leading product design innovation at startups, venture studios, consulting and enterprise businesses.

I have had the incredible opportunity to attempt solving some big and small problems across financial services, drone tech, education, healthcare, open banking, ecommerce, and SaaS. In addition, I have designed open-source tools used by over 20,000 users, cofounded a UX Research Lab, and helped businesses raise over $750,000 in pre-seed funding. I am also a recipient of UK's Global Talent Award for "Exceptional Talent & Leadership in Technology".
After 8 years of doing all that, I am changing careers to pursue a long-held childhood dream of taking to the skies. Currently, I am pursuing an MSc in Safety and Human Factors in Aviation at Cranfield University. My broad research interest is in developing Human-AI systems for future flight and predictive safety systems.
